Place your background video here as:

  background.mov   (primary — .mov, 4K recommended)
  background.mp4   (fallback — browser-compatible)

The site will automatically use it as a looping background on the home page.

Tips for best results:
- Resolution: 3840x2160 (4K) or 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Duration: 10–30 seconds loops best
- Keep file size under 50MB for fast loading
- Use a dark/aerial drone footage clip for best visual match
- To convert .mov to .mp4: ffmpeg -i background.mov -vcodec h264 -acodec aac background.mp4
